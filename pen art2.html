Mandala Art 2

Art information

  • Category: Art
  • Date: 01 April, 2020

When I began drawing the mandalas, I saw that everything, all the parts I had been following, all the steps I had taken were leading back to a single point- namely, the midpoint. It became increasingly plain to me that the mandala is the center. It is the exponent of all the parts. It is the path to the center to individuation.
